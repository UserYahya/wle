Muhammad Yahya

Chief Organiser, Wiki Loves Earth 2025 in Bangladesh

A passionate open knowledge advocate, Wikimedia leader, and experienced community organizer from Bangladesh. Dedicated to capturing and celebrating the nation's natural beauty on a global stage.

Key Initiatives

Chief Organiser of Wiki Loves Earth 2025

Leading the national campaign to promote open environmental knowledge through photography and enrich Wikimedia Commons.

Chief Coordinator of Dhaka Wikimedia Community

Leading outreach, events, GLAM, and growth across the capital

Founder of Bangla WikiConnect

Spearheading year-long campaigns to activate regional contributors and grow the Wikimedia community in Bangladesh.

Community Builder

Actively bridging the gap between newcomers and experienced Wikimedians, with a focus on supporting the Global South.

Roles in the Wikimedia Movement

  • Wikimedia Steward: A globally trusted user supporting community governance across all Wikimedia wikis.
  • Administrator: Holds admin rights on multiple projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, and Wikidata.
  • Campaign Organiser: Leads and supports global initiatives like Wikipedia Asian Month, Wiki Loves Monuments, and more.
  • Project Growth: Focuses on expanding smaller and underrepresented Wikimedia projects such as Wikivoyage and Wiktionary.

Conference Involvement

  • Wikimania Bangladesh 2022: Served as a Core Organising Team Member for the flagship global Wikimedia conference's local edition.
  • Bangla Wikiconference 2024: Acted as Operations Team Member and Communications Manager for the major regional conference.
