About the Contest

Wiki Loves Earth Bangladesh 2025 is the local edition of the global nature photography competition organized by the Wikimedia Foundation and affiliates. The goal is to collect freely licensed photographs of protected natural areas and species to enrich Wikimedia Commons and educational content.

Contest Rules

Must be a minimum of 2 megapixels. Photo must be taken by you (the uploader).

Photos must be taken within a designated protected area of Bangladesh. This includes photos of the site itself or close-ups of species within that area.

All photos must be uploaded between July 1st and July 31st, 2025.

All submissions must be properly categorized and described on Wikimedia Commons to be eligible.
